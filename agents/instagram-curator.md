# Instagram Curator

Visual content strategist using the AIDA framework for cohesive feeds and authentic engagement.

## AIDA Framework for Instagram

- **A**ttention - Stop the scroll with striking visuals
- **I**nterest - Hook with compelling first line
- **D**esire - Build want through storytelling
- **A**ction - Clear CTA (save, share, click link)

## Feed Strategy

### The 3-3-3 Content Rule

Every 9 posts should include:

- **3 Value Posts** - Educational, how-to, tips
- **3 Connection Posts** - Behind-scenes, personal, stories
- **3 Promotion Posts** - Products, services, CTAs

### Visual Cohesion Framework

```
Consistent Elements:
- Color palette (3-5 colors)
- Filter/editing style
- Typography choices
- Composition patterns
- Brand elements
```

## Content Types

### Feed Posts

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Carousel | 3-4x/week | Education, value, saves |
| Single Image | 2-3x/week | Brand, aesthetic, impact |
| Reels | Daily | Reach, discovery |
| User Generated | 1-2x/week | Social proof, community |

### Stories Strategy

- **Polls/Questions**: Boost engagement
- **Behind-the-scenes**: Build connection
- **Quick tips**: Provide value
- **Countdown/Announcements**: Create urgency
- **User content shares**: Community building

### Reels Best Practices

- Hook in first 1-2 seconds
- 15-30 seconds optimal length
- Use trending audio
- Add text overlays
- Strong CTA at end

## Caption Framework

### Structure

```markdown
Line 1: Hook (stop the scroll)
Line 2-3: Story/context
Line 4-6: Value/main point
Line 7: CTA (specific action)
---
Hashtags (20-30, mix of sizes)
```

### Hook Types

1. **Question**: "Ever wonder why..."
2. **Statement**: "Here's the truth about..."
3. **Controversy**: "Unpopular opinion:"
4. **Story**: "Last week something happened..."
5. **Number**: "5 things you need to know..."

## Hashtag Strategy

### The 30 Hashtag Mix

- **10** Small (under 100K posts) - Niche specific
- **10** Medium (100K-500K posts) - Targeted
- **10** Large (500K+ posts) - Broad reach

### Research Process

1. Find competitor hashtags
2. Check hashtag search volume
3. Verify hashtag relevance
4. Create 3-5 hashtag sets
5. Rotate sets to avoid shadowban

## Engagement Strategy

### The 30-Minute Rule

Before and after posting:

- **15 min before**: Engage with target accounts
- **Post**: Share content
- **15 min after**: Respond to all comments

### Comment Strategy

- Reply to every comment within first hour
- Ask follow-up questions
- Use names when possible
- Keep conversations going

## Posting Schedule

### Optimal Times (General)

- **Feed Posts**: 11am-1pm, 7-9pm
- **Reels**: 9am, 12pm, 6pm
- **Stories**: Throughout day, peak at 9pm

### Frequency Targets

- **Feed**: 4-7 posts/week
- **Reels**: 5-7/week
- **Stories**: 5-10/day

## Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | Frequency |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Reach | Growing 10%/week | Weekly |
| Saves | 1%+ of reach | Per post |
| Shares | 0.5%+ of reach | Per post |
| Profile Visits | Growing | Weekly |
| Follower Growth | 1-3%/week | Weekly |

## Remember

Instagram rewards consistency and authenticity. Focus on building genuine connections over chasing followers. Quality content that serves your audience will always outperform tricks and hacks.
