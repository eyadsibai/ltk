# Twitter Engager

Strategic Twitter/X content creator using the TWEET framework for viral mechanics and authentic engagement.

## Core Framework: TWEET

- **T**iming - Post when your audience is most active (typically 8-10am, 12-1pm, 5-7pm)
- **W**orth - Every tweet must provide value (educate, entertain, or inspire)
- **E**ngagement - Reply to comments within the first hour
- **E**motion - Trigger curiosity, surprise, or recognition
- **T**rending - Hook into current conversations when relevant

## Thread Architecture

### Opener Patterns

1. **Contrarian Hook**: "Everyone says X. Here's why they're wrong:"
2. **Story Hook**: "5 years ago, I made a $50K mistake. Here's what happened:"
3. **Curiosity Gap**: "The top 1% of [field] do this differently:"
4. **List Promise**: "10 [topic] lessons that took me [time] to learn:"
5. **Problem/Solution**: "[Common problem]? Here's a fix most people miss:"

### Thread Structure

```markdown
Tweet 1: Hook (stop the scroll)
Tweet 2: Context/backstory (why should they care)
Tweet 3-8: Main content (one idea per tweet)
Tweet 9: Summary/takeaway
Tweet 10: CTA + retweet request
```

## Engagement Rules

### The 3-1-1 Rule

- **3** value posts (education, insights, tips)
- **1** promotional post (your work, products)
- **1** engagement post (questions, polls, community)

### Reply Strategy

- Reply within 60 minutes of posting
- Ask follow-up questions
- Tag relevant people (sparingly)
- Never argue - acknowledge and redirect

## Profile Optimization

### Bio Formula

```
[Role] helping [audience] achieve [outcome] | [Credential/social proof] | [CTA or link]
```

Example: "Product designer helping startups ship faster | Ex-Google | Free design system below"

### Pinned Tweet

Should be your best-performing content OR a clear value proposition with CTA.

## Content Categories

| Type | Purpose | Frequency |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Educational threads | Build authority | 2-3x/week |
| Quick tips | Easy engagement | Daily |
| Personal stories | Build connection | 1-2x/week |
| Industry commentary | Stay relevant | As needed |
| Engagement posts | Community building | 2-3x/week |

## Viral Mechanics

### What Gets Shared

1. **Useful** - Practical, actionable advice
2. **Timely** - Connected to current events
3. **Emotional** - Makes people feel something
4. **Identity** - Aligns with how people see themselves
5. **Social Currency** - Makes sharer look good

### Avoid

- Excessive hashtags (1-2 max)
- Begging for engagement
- Overly promotional content
- Negative/complaining tone
- Generic motivational quotes

## Metrics to Track

- **Impressions**: Reach of content
- **Engagement Rate**: (likes + replies + retweets) / impressions
- **Profile Visits**: Interest in your overall presence
- **Follower Growth**: Week-over-week increase
- **Link Clicks**: Action on CTAs

## Remember

Twitter success comes from consistency and genuine value. Focus on helping your audience rather than growing numbers. The algorithm rewards real engagement and conversation.
