# Social Media Marketing Guide

Social media strategy, content planning, and analytics.

## When to Use

- Planning social media strategy
- Creating content calendars
- Analyzing engagement metrics
- Optimizing posting schedules
- Measuring social ROI

## Platform Strategy

### Platform Comparison

| Platform | Best For | Content Type | Posting Frequency |
|----------|----------|--------------|-------------------|
| LinkedIn | B2B, thought leadership | Articles, insights | 1-2/day |
| Twitter | Tech, news, engagement | Threads, updates | 3-5/day |
| Instagram | Visual brands, lifestyle | Images, reels | 1-2/day |
| TikTok | Gen Z, entertainment | Short video | 1-3/day |
| YouTube | Education, tutorials | Long video | 1-2/week |

### Audience Fit

| Platform | Primary Audience | Professional Use |
|----------|------------------|------------------|
| LinkedIn | 25-54, professionals | High |
| Twitter | 18-49, tech-savvy | High |
| Instagram | 18-34, consumers | Medium |
| TikTok | 16-30, entertainment | Low-Medium |
| Facebook | 30-65, broad | Medium |

## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars

```
1. Educational (40%)
   - How-tos, tips, insights

2. Engaging (30%)
   - Questions, polls, discussions

3. Promotional (20%)
   - Product, features, updates

4. Cultural (10%)
   - Behind-the-scenes, team
```

### Content Calendar Template

| Day | Platform | Content Type | Topic | CTA |
|-----|----------|--------------|-------|-----|
| Mon | LinkedIn | Article | Industry insight | Comment |
| Tue | Twitter | Thread | Tips series | Retweet |
| Wed | Instagram | Carousel | Product feature | Link |
| Thu | LinkedIn | Poll | Engagement | Vote |
| Fri | Twitter | Discussion | Community | Reply |

## Engagement Metrics

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Engagement Rate | (Likes+Comments+Shares)/Impressions | 1-3% |
| Click-Through Rate | Clicks/Impressions | 0.5-2% |
| Share Rate | Shares/Impressions | 0.1-0.5% |
| Follower Growth | New Followers/Total | 2-5%/month |

### Platform Benchmarks

| Metric | LinkedIn | Twitter | Instagram |
|--------|----------|---------|-----------|
| Engagement | 0.5-2% | 0.5-1% | 1-3% |
| CTR | 0.5-1% | 1-2% | 0.5-1% |
| Video views | 10-15% | 5-10% | 20-30% |

## Posting Best Practices

### Optimal Posting Times

| Platform | Best Times (Local) |
|----------|-------------------|
| LinkedIn | Tue-Thu, 8-10am, 12pm |
| Twitter | Mon-Fri, 8-10am, 12-1pm |
| Instagram | Mon-Fri, 11am-1pm, 7-9pm |
| Facebook | Tue-Thu, 1-4pm |

### Post Structure

**LinkedIn:**

```
[Hook - first line visible]

[2-3 paragraphs of value]

[Call to action]

#hashtag1 #hashtag2 #hashtag3
```

**Twitter Thread:**

```
1/ [Hook that creates curiosity]

2-8/ [Value content]

9/ [Summary and CTA]

[Retweet prompt]
```
