# CEO Advisory Guide

Strategic decision-making frameworks for executive leadership.

## When to Use

- Making strategic company decisions
- Preparing board presentations
- Planning fundraising rounds
- Evaluating M&A opportunities
- Designing organizational structure

## Strategic Decision Framework

### Decision Matrix

| Factor | Weight | Option A | Option B |
|--------|--------|----------|----------|
| Strategic alignment | 30% | Score | Score |
| Financial impact | 25% | Score | Score |
| Risk level | 20% | Score | Score |
| Execution complexity | 15% | Score | Score |
| Team capability | 10% | Score | Score |

### Decision Types

| Type | Timeframe | Reversibility | Process |
|------|-----------|---------------|---------|
| Strategic | 1-5 years | Low | Deep analysis |
| Operational | Quarters | Medium | Quick evaluation |
| Tactical | Days-weeks | High | Delegate |

## Fundraising Framework

### Stage Benchmarks

| Stage | Typical Raise | Valuation | Metrics |
|-------|---------------|-----------|---------|
| Pre-seed | $500K-1M | $5-10M | Vision + team |
| Seed | $2-4M | $10-25M | Early traction |
| Series A | $10-20M | $40-80M | Product-market fit |
| Series B | $30-60M | $100-300M | Scalable growth |

### Pitch Structure

1. **Problem** (1 slide)
2. **Solution** (1-2 slides)
3. **Market** (1 slide)
4. **Traction** (1-2 slides)
5. **Business Model** (1 slide)
6. **Team** (1 slide)
7. **Financials** (1-2 slides)
8. **Ask** (1 slide)

### Due Diligence Prep

- [ ] Cap table clean and current
- [ ] Financial model (3 years)
- [ ] Legal documents organized
- [ ] Customer references ready
- [ ] Key metrics documented

## Board Management

### Meeting Structure

```
1. Approve previous minutes (5 min)
2. CEO update (20 min)
   - Key metrics vs plan
   - Strategic progress
   - Challenges/risks
3. Deep dive topic (30 min)
4. Financial review (15 min)
5. Closed session (as needed)
6. Action items (5 min)
```

### Board Deck Template

1. Executive summary
2. KPI dashboard
3. Financial performance
4. Strategic initiatives update
5. Risk assessment
6. Key decisions needed
7. Appendix

## Organizational Design

### Span of Control

| Role Level | Typical Reports |
|------------|-----------------|
| C-Level | 5-8 |
| VP | 6-10 |
| Director | 8-12 |
| Manager | 8-15 |

### Org Evolution

| Stage | Structure | Focus |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| <20 people | Flat | Speed |
| 20-50 | Functional | Specialization |
| 50-150 | Departmental | Coordination |
| 150+ | Business units | Scale |

## M&A Evaluation

### Acquisition Framework

| Factor | Questions |
|--------|-----------|
| Strategic Fit | Does it accelerate strategy? |
| Financial | What's the ROI timeline? |
| Cultural | Can we integrate? |
| Technical | Is the stack compatible? |
| Talent | Will key people stay? |

### Deal Structure

| Type | When to Use |
|------|-------------|
| Cash | Quick close, simple |
| Stock | Align incentives |
| Earnout | Risk mitigation |
| Mix | Balance interests |

## Crisis Management

### Response Framework

1. **Assess** - Understand scope and impact
2. **Contain** - Limit immediate damage
3. **Communicate** - Transparent updates
4. **Resolve** - Address root cause
5. **Learn** - Post-mortem and prevention

### Stakeholder Communication

| Audience | Priority | Channel |
|----------|----------|---------|
| Board | Immediate | Direct call |
| Employees | Within hours | All-hands |
| Customers | Same day | Email/status |
| Public | As needed | PR/social |

## Leadership Principles

### Decision Making

- Seek diverse perspectives
- Make decisions with incomplete info
- Communicate reasoning
- Own outcomes

### Team Building

- Hire for values fit
- Develop leadership pipeline
- Give autonomy with accountability
- Celebrate wins, learn from failures

### Communication

- Be transparent by default
- Repeat key messages
- Listen more than talk
- Admit what you don't know
